Delicious Ham and Potato Soup

Introduction

Ham and Potato Soup is a hearty, comforting dish that combines the creamy texture of potatoes with the savory flavor of ham. Simmered with onions, carrots, and other vegetables, this soup delivers a rich and flavorful broth. Perfect for warming up on a cold day or serving as a satisfying starter at the family table.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables: onion, celery, and potatoes
  • Water: boil the ham and veggies in water
  • Ham
  • Seansonings: chicken bouillon granules, salt, and black pepper
  • Butter and flour: make a roux with butter and all-purpose flour
  • Milk: whisk two cups of milk into the roux of a creamy soup base

Directions

Click here to get a detailed video tutorial.
  • Step 1
    Gather all ingredients.


  • Step 2
    Combine potatoes, water, ham, celery, and onion in a stockpot. Bring to a boil; simmer over medium heat until potatoes are tender, 10 to 15 minutes. Stor in chicken bouillon, pepper, and salt.


  • Step 3
    Melt butter in a separate saucepan over medium-low heat. Add flour; cook and stir until thick, about 1 minute. Whisk in milk; cook and stir until thick, 4 to 5 minutes.


  • Step 4
    Pour milk mixture into the stockpot; cook and stir until warmed through.


  • Step 5
    Serve and enjoy!


How to Store Ham and Potato Soup?

Store the ham and potato soup in an airtight storage container for up to four days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stove.
